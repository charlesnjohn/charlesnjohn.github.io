About Me

Hello, I am Charles. I have varied interests among many fields. I believe technology in the hands of the good makes peoples' lives better. It can be tyrannical in the hands of bad actors. My work in AI isn't just about building smarter systems—it's about creating tools that genuinely improve lives.

From developing diagnostic tools during COVID-19 to creating safety systems for industrial workers, I've seen how thoughtful technology can make a real difference. But I've also learned that the most meaningful impact often happens when technology is used as a tool to better the community rather than the made master that rules.

I enjoy spending time building relationships with international students and exploring ideas through writing as I do training neural networks. I love to invest in the lives of kids to make wonderful citizens and leaders. Good technology serves people, but great technology builds communities.

Ministry & Community

Since I was a kid I had involved in ministry in some form. My devout Hindu grandfather came to know Christ when he was young through a miraculous healing in his body. All his devotion turned then to Lord Jesus and started following Him and gave his life to serve Him and people. Growing up with him, it became part of my life to help people and involved in ministry at some capacity. These days my work in ministry focuses on creating spaces where international students can find belonging and local communities can experience the richness of global perspectives.
